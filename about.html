About COGNIZANCE

COGNIZANCE is the annual technical festival of CHARUSAT...

Our Mission

At COGNIZANCE, we strive to create a platform where technology enthusiasts, researchers, and industry leaders converge to explore the cutting edge of artificial intelligence and robotics.

Our mission is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and showcase groundbreaking technologies that will shape the future of humanity.

This year's edition focuses on practical applications of AI in robotics, with hands-on workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities.

Our Journey

2015

Inception

COGNIZANCE was born as a small technical event at CHARUSAT with just 5 competitions and 200 participants.

2017

First Major Sponsorship

Secured our first corporate sponsorship and expanded to 15 events with 800+ participants.

2019

International Recognition

Featured our first international speaker and crossed 2000 participants from 5 countries.

2021

Virtual Transition

Successfully transitioned to a fully virtual format during the pandemic, reaching 5000+ attendees globally.

2023

Record Participation

Broke all records with 10,000+ participants and 50+ events in hybrid format.

2026

COGNIZANCE 26

Our biggest edition yet, focusing exclusively on AI and Robotics with world-class speakers and competitions.
